Portfolio

Here you can view some of my previous work for a variety of local businesses and charities

Evington Muslim Centre

The Evington Muslim Centre is an award-winning local Mosque based in Evington, Leicester. The website needed to be designed to accommodate a high number of visitors. The website features advanced CSS to provide an excellent graphic user interface. Key information such as prayer timetables and service information are easily available.


The Eye Clinic of Northampton

The Eye Clinic of Northampton is a local opticians based in Northampton. The design requirements for this site included a professional sleek design, that highlights the modern approach the clinic takes to optometry. Key information is readily accessible, and users of the site are also able to book appointments online.


Evington Community Centre

Evington Community Centre is a local function room for events such as conferences and weddings. The website uses Revolution Slider to showcase the excellent facilities that are available on site.

The Web Design Process

Briefing and requirements
We start by visiting our client and asking a variety of open-ended questions to understand how their business works. This stage involved understanding the brand message for the client and providing solutions that meet the client expectation

Design Proposals
We produce a series of design proposals based on our briefing. The designs showcase the key features of the website. The client chooses the design they prefer

Development
We convert the design into usable code that displays in a browser. This stage involves implementing custom solutions using CSS and HTML.

Design Revisions
We provide the client with the opportunity to review the design and suggest improvements or new features. This cycle repeats a few times until the client is satisfied with the final result.

Testing and Release
We test all the functionality of the website, and any necessary changes are made. The textual content is reviewed and proof-read before we publish the site.
