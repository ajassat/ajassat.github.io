My Hobbies

Chess

I enjoy playing chess in my free time. I enjoy the strategic element of chess. It helps to develop strategic thinking skills and concentration which can be applied to many areas outside of chess itself.

Formula One

I enjoy watching Formula One, and I have followed the sport since 1999. I am interested because of the highly technical nature of the sport, and its reliance on lots of technical data to improve performance on the track.

Coffee

I am a big fan of coffee. I enjoy smelling and tasting a variety of different coffee's. my primary method of brewing is the grind beans and use them in a French Press coffee maker. I find this results in a strong, full bodied flavour to be enjoyed any time of the day.

Fine Dining

I enjoy eating out, and my favourite cuisines are Indian, Italian, and Turkish. For me, good food is one thing, but the restaurant ambience and atmosphere also adds to the overall experience.
